Fascinated by all things audio, looking to develop innovative software, design sound and compose music for live and recorded media.

Entering my 3rd year at the University of Leeds in Music, Multimedia and Electronics (BSc), I am focusing on sound design, composition and development of interactive performance and embedded systems. In practice this translates to linking hardware and microcontrollers such as the Kinect and Arduino with software languages such as MaxMSP and C++, as well developing skill in DAWs such as Ableton Live and Pro Tools making use of personally recorded material (harp, foley).

This website was made to showcase works, document my research process, and track ongoing projects .
